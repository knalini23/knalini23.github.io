15 Day Return Policy

* Receive a refund if you return a product within 15 days of your delivery date that is till in brand new condition with the original packaging and with all items delivered with the product.

  • You have 15 days to return the product from the date you received it.
  • To be eligible for a replacement or refund, your item must be unused and in the same consition and packaging that you received it in,
  • The rreplacement or refund will be initiated when the product is received back to the facility.
  • Then, as per banking and payment norms, your refund will be transferred within 3 to 5 business days.

    • Missing/Damaged Fee

    For products returned with the following conditions below, a 15% product missing/damaged fee will be deducted from the refunded amount.

    • Wear and tear on the product like small scratches and dents
    • Damage to the original manufacturing box or accessories
    • Missing box items like an owner's manual or software/ driver CD's, etc.
    • Damage or dents incurred during the return shipping process
    • Tampered / Missing barcode from the product

    Note: In case NoteNexon is unable to pick-up your shipment due to certain unforseen circumstance, you may have to ship the products to our facility. The logistic charges involved in these cases would be refunded back to you after the product reaches us.
